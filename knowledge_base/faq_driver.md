# Za6zo Driver FAQ

## How do I become a Za6zo driver?
Visit za6zo.com and click "Become a Driver" or use the app.
Complete your registration, submit required documents,
and get verified to start earning.

## Does Za6zo take commission from drivers?
No. Za6zo charges zero commission. Every rupee a passenger pays
goes directly to you, the driver. This is Za6zo's biggest advantage
over Uber, Careem, and InDrive.

## How much can I earn on Za6zo?
Example: 10 rides/day at Rs 500 average fare = Rs 5,000/day,
Rs 35,000/week, Rs 150,000/month. Other apps take 25% commission
(Rs 37,500/month lost). On Za6zo, you keep it all.

## What does Za6zo charge drivers?
Za6zo charges a small monthly subscription fee — not a per-ride commission.
The first month is completely free.

## What documents do I need to register as a driver?
Standard vehicle and identity documents are required.
Contact Za6zo support or visit za6zo.com/driver for the exact list.

## Can any vehicle type join Za6zo?
Yes. Za6zo accepts Sedans, Minis, Bikes, Rickshaws, Vans, Pickups,
and Buses. Different vehicle types serve different ride categories.

## How do I receive payments?
Passengers pay driver fares directly. Za6zo does not take a cut.
Payment method details are in the driver app.

## Is the first month really free?
Yes. Your first month as a Za6zo driver is completely free.
After that, only a small monthly subscription applies.
