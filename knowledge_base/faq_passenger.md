# Za6zo Passenger FAQ

## What is Za6zo?
Za6zo is Pakistan's trusted ride-sharing app. Tap, ride, done — no haggling,
no drama. Clean rides, verified drivers, and fares with no surge pricing ever.
Available across Punjab, Sindh, KPK, Balochistan, Gilgit-Baltistan, AJK, and Islamabad.

## Is Za6zo free for passengers?
Yes. Za6zo is completely free for passengers — Rs 0 forever.
There are no hidden charges, no surge pricing, and no platform fees.
You only pay the driver's fare directly.

## How do I book a ride?
Download the Za6zo app from the App Store or Google Play.
Open the app, enter your pickup and destination, choose your vehicle type,
and confirm your booking. A verified driver will be assigned to you.

## What vehicle types are available?
Za6zo offers 7 vehicle types:
- Sedan: AC comfort for daily use
- Mini: Budget-friendly small car
- Bike: Beat traffic fast
- Rickshaw: Shortcut specialist
- Van: For groups and families
- Pickup: For heavy loads and cargo
- Bus: For large groups

## Can I schedule a ride in advance?
Yes. Za6zo offers Scheduled Rides — book hours or even days in advance.
Your driver will show up exactly when you need them. No last-minute stress.

## Does Za6zo do intercity / city-to-city rides?
Yes. Za6zo offers City to City rides for long-distance travel between Pakistani cities.
Comfortable, affordable, and on your schedule. Great for airport pickups too.

## What is the monthly subscription for passengers?
Za6zo offers a Monthly Subscription plan where you pay one fixed monthly fee
and ride daily without worrying about per-ride charges. Perfect for daily commuters.

## Are shared rides available?
Yes. Shared Rides let you share a vehicle with others going the same direction
and split the cost. Smart and affordable.

## Is Za6zo safe?
Yes. All Za6zo drivers are verified. Za6zo focuses on safety for all riders
including school children, female students, and families.

## Can I use Za6zo for my child's school pickup/drop?
Yes. Za6zo offers School Rickshaw — a monthly service with the same verified
driver, same route, every single day. Parents can relax knowing their child
is safe.

## What cities does Za6zo operate in?
Za6zo covers all provinces of Pakistan: Punjab, Sindh, KPK, Balochistan,
Gilgit-Baltistan, AJK, and Islamabad. The service is expanding fast to
every city across Pakistan.

## How do I download the Za6zo app?
Download Za6zo from the Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
Search for "Za6zo" and install for free.

## Is there surge pricing?
No. Za6zo has a strict no surge pricing policy. Fares never spike
during rain, rush hour, or busy events.

## How do I contact Za6zo support?
You can contact Za6zo through this support chat, through the app,
or via the website at za6zo.com.
