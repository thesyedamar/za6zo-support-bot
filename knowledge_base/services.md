# Za6zo Services Overview

## On-Demand Rides
Instant rides available across Pakistan. Open the app and get a driver fast.

## Scheduled Rides  
Book in advance — hours or days ahead. Driver arrives on time, guaranteed.

## Monthly Subscription
Fixed monthly fee. Ride every day without per-trip charges.
Best for daily office commuters and students.

## City to City (Intercity)
Long-distance rides between Pakistani cities. Airport transfers, family trips,
business travel. Comfortable and affordable.

## Shared Rides
Share your ride with other passengers going the same direction.
Split the cost. Available in major cities.

## School Rickshaw (Monthly)
Dedicated rickshaw service for school children. Same verified driver,
same route, every school day. Parents get peace of mind.

## Monthly Commute for Students
Daily rides to university and back. Safe, reliable, affordable for students.

## Special/Family Rides
Weddings, family outings, airport trips — book a vehicle for the whole group.
