# Za6zo Pricing Model

## Passenger Pricing
- Platform fee: Rs 0 (free forever)
- Surge pricing: None, ever
- Hidden charges: None
- You pay: Driver's fare only

## Driver Pricing
- Commission: 0% (zero commission)
- First month: Completely free
- After first month: Small fixed monthly subscription
- You keep: 100% of every fare

## Example Driver Earnings (zero commission model)
- Rides per day: 10
- Average fare: Rs 500
- Daily earnings: Rs 5,000
- Weekly earnings: Rs 35,000
- Monthly earnings: Rs 150,000
- What competitors take (25%): Rs 37,500/month
- What Za6zo takes: Rs 0

## Why Za6zo is different
Other apps like Careem (before exit) and InDrive take 20-25% commission
per ride. Za6zo charges only a small monthly subscription to drivers,
meaning drivers earn significantly more on Za6zo.
